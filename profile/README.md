## üöÄ About OptiCool
Welcome to Bangkit Product-Based Capstone Project of Team CH2-PS291. Introducing OptiCool, it is a mobile application designed to boost individuals' confidence in wearing glasses by offering personalized recommendations for eyeglass frames that perfectly complement their unique face shapes. Beyond merely providing advice on glasses, our mission is to instil greater confidence in users' overall appearance through the selection of frames that harmonize with their facial features. Leveraging s AI technology, OptiCool assists users in identifying their specific face types, ensuring a tailored and confidence-boosting eyewear experience.
